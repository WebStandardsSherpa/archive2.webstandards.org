Chris Casciano

Chris Casciano is a freelance web developer based just outside NYC. When he's not up late debugging code and drinking black coffee he writes about web technologies for his site ChunkySoup.net. Turn-ons include good music, good photography, and long moonlit strolls on the boardwalk.
